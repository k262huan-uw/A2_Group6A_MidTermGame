A2: Midterm Game - Boba Bar Monster Mayhem

Group6A

Description: Brief overview of your game’s concept, mechanics, and player experience.
Our games concept is to help players understand colour vision deficiency by showing how colours may appear to someone with the disability. Players prepare orders by matching customer orders from selecting a base, syrup, and topping. The player starts off by seeing a preview phase of the customers order with normal vision, shortly after the ordering system appears and switches to the colour vision deficiency mode. This makes it more challenging for players to complete the order withiin the time limit. In a way, mimicing what it may be like to experience colour deficiency as the colours are harder to distinguish.

Setup and Interaction Instructions: How to run and play the game.
You run a bubble tea counter for mochi monsters.

Mechanic 1:
Player sees the order briefly in normal vision with bright colours.

Mechanic 2:
Order menu appears, switching the order colours to the colour deficiency/CVD mode.

Mechanic 3:
Player can click between ingredient options, trying to accurately match options with the order.

How to make a drink:

1. Choose 1 Tea Base, 1 Syrup, 1 Topping
2. Click SERVE before time runs out

Mechanic 4:
Serve button, after selecting chosen ingredients.

1. SERVE BEFORE timer runs out with correct toppings = win points/tips, increased score
2. SERVE BEFORE timer but with incorrect toppings = lose, no points
3. UNABLE TO SERVE BEFORE timer runs out = lose, no points, try again

Keys:
R = restart
